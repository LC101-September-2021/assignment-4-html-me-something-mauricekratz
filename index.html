Elements

The world is composed of various elements crafted by mother nature herself. Each one has it's own strength. Choose wisely for it may determine your destiny...

Fire

Choose fire to illuminate the world. Those around you see how brightly you burn, but be careful not to illuminate too brightly or your may find yourself blinded by your own light.

Water

One of the fundamental natures of life, water has cleansing properties. Best known for feats like rain and snow this element makes up most of Earth.

Earth

Known for stabilty, this element is in charge of all the wonderful green that we see on this planet. The amazing biodiversity that exist on this planet is awe inspiring.

Wind

The very definition of head in the clouds. Life a lofty and aiming ever higher. Whether caused by the sun or changes in the pressure system you're sure to experience wonder.
